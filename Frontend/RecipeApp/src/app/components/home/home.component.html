Have a lot of things in your kitchen you don't know what to do with? This application is designed with you in mind. Get inspired with ResApi (pronounced like recipe)! No more wondering what to cook or thinking you don't have anything to make. Let ResApi do the work for you by suggesting recipes tailored to you.

Save Your Ingredients & Recipes Be a Member of the Family

Not only can you take inventory of what is in your own kitchen, but you can keep track of it by creating an account and saving your list of ingredients to come back to each time you log in. Keep track of your food, save recipes you like, and stay excited about cooking!

Delicious Recipes, Good Eats Kitchen & Pantry

Use this application to keep track of your ingredients. Based on what you add to your virtual "pantry," you will get a list of suggested recipes to prepare. Use the "Add Ingredients" tab to start putting together your own pantry.
