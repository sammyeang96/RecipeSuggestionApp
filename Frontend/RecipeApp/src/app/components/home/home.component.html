Delicious Recipes From Your Kitchen

Cooking is hard when you don't know what you're doing. You probably went shopping and bought a bunch of food you know is going to go bad, but you don't know what to do with any of it. Use this application to keep track of your ingredients. Based on what you add to your virtual "pantry," you will get a list of suggested recipes to prepare. Use the "Add Ingredients" tab to start putting together your own pantry.
